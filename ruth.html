Topics & Problems Ruth Tackles

Admin, security, and data management challenges

01

Salesforce Security Model

OWD, Profiles, Roles, Permission Sets, Sharing Rules — designing the right access model so users see exactly what they should, nothing more.

02

User Management

Creating and deactivating users, managing licenses, SSO, login hours, and IP restrictions. Right people, right access, from day one to day done.

03

Data Quality & Deduplication

Duplicate rules, matching rules, import tools, and data loader strategies. Clean data = a fast org. Debt compounds silently until it breaks everything.

04

Backup & Data Recovery

Scheduled exports, Salesforce Backup, recycle bin management, and recovery from mass-delete or accidental field truncation before anyone notices.

05

Reports & Dashboards

Tabular, summary, matrix, and joined reports. Scheduled refreshes, subscriptions, and leadership pipeline visibility — no manual pulls needed.

06

Change Management & Releases

Sandbox → UAT → Production pipelines. Change sets, validation runs, go-live checklists, and communicating changes so releases don't break users' workflows.
