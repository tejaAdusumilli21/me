Topics & Problems Astro Tackles

The real-world Salesforce challenges that fall under Astro's domain

01

Trailhead Learning Paths

New users don't know where to start. Astro defines structured trails — Admin, Developer, Architect — so learning feels like a journey, not a wall of docs.

02

Onboarding New Users

Getting first-time Salesforce users comfortable: navigating the UI, understanding objects, setting up their first record.

03

Certification Roadmaps

Which cert to go for first? Admin, PD1, App Builder? Astro represents the journey of mapping out cert paths and knowing which badge matters for which role.

04

Org Navigation & Setup

Understanding Setup menus, App Manager, object relationships, and how Salesforce orgs are structured — all foundational exploration problems Astro owns.

05

Community Engagement

Finding user groups, Dreamforce, Trailblazer Community forums — connecting learners to the Ohana so they don't feel alone when stuck on a governor limit at 2am.

06

Platform Exploration & New Features

Each Salesforce release adds 200+ features. Astro's spirit drives the habit of reading release notes, exploring sandboxes, and staying current without getting overwhelmed.
