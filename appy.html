Topics & Problems Appy Tackles

Declarative automation and app-building challenges

01

Flow Builder Automation

Replacing Process Builder and Workflow Rules with Flows — record-triggered, scheduled, screen, and autolaunched flows that handle complex branching logic without Apex.

02

Lightning App Builder

Building custom Lightning pages — Home, Record, App — by dragging components onto a canvas and configuring them for specific profiles, devices, and record types.

03

Custom Object & Field Design

Modelling business requirements as Salesforce objects, fields, and relationships. Knowing when to use a custom object vs a junction object vs a standard object extension.

04

Validation Rules & Formula Fields

Enforcing data quality with validation rules, building complex cross-object formulas, and using rollup summary fields to aggregate child data on parent records.

05

AppExchange Package Management

Evaluating, installing, and configuring managed packages — understanding licensing, upgrade paths, and how package objects interact with custom org data.

06

Dynamic Forms & Actions

Using Dynamic Forms to show/hide fields based on criteria, and Dynamic Actions to surface the right buttons at the right time — without hard-coding page layouts per profile.
